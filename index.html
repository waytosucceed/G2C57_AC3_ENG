What did I learn?

Persuasive writing is a way to share your thoughts and try to convince others to agree with you. It is important because it helps you express your opinion and give reasons to support it.
When you write persuasively, you can persuade your friends, family, or even teachers to see things your way!

The elements of persuasive writing are what make it strong and convincing.

  • First, you start by stating your opinion—what you believe is true.
  • Then, you give reasons and examples to show why your opinion is right.
  • Using exciting words can make your writing even stronger, so your reader feels excited to agree with you.
  • Finally, you end with a conclusion that reminds the reader of your main idea, leaving them convinced.

Persuasive writing is an important skill because it helps you share your ideas and make people understand how you feel. Whether you are writing about your favourite book, hobby, or anything else, these elements will help you make your writing powerful and persuasive!
